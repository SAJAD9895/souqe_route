Who We Are

SouqRoute is Saudi Arabia's pioneering digital platform revolutionizing the industrial supply chain ecosystem. We connect verified suppliers with buyers through an intelligent, transparent, and efficient marketplace that supports the Kingdom's Vision 2030 transformation goals.

Founded on the principles of trust, innovation, and excellence, we bridge the gap between traditional industrial procurement and modern digital solutions, creating unprecedented value for all stakeholders in Saudi Arabia's industrial sector.
